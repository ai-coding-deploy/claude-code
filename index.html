Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2025 | NovaLume Labs believes in transparent, gentle data practices.

1. Information we collect

We collect basic account information (name, email), conversation history (encrypted), and usage patterns. You own everything you create. We never train public models on your private data.

2. How we use your information

To provide AI services, improve our models, personalize your experience, billing, and support. No selling or sharing for advertising.

3. Data retention & deletion

You can delete conversations anytime. Account data retained until deletion requested. Full data export available.

4. Third-party sharing

We use trusted infrastructure (AWS, Cloudflare) under strict data agreements. All data encrypted at rest and in transit.

5. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability. Email privacy@novalume.ai. Marketing opt-out available.

6. Security measures

AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3, regular audits, and privacy-by-design.

7. Children's privacy

NovaLume is intended for users 16+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. International transfers

Data may be processed globally with Standard Contractual Clauses safeguards.

9. Policy updates & contact

Material changes notified via email. Data Protection Officer: dpo@novalume.ai. Address: 101 Glow Lane, Portland, OR 97205.
